A simple, private PDF editor — your file never leaves your machine.

How to use

  • Open a PDF (button or drag & drop).
  • Edit — click any line and type to change it in place.
  • Text — type, then click the page to drop new text.
  • Sign — draw, type, or upload a signature in the popup, then drag it into place.
  • Erase — drag a box over anything (e.g. an old signature) to remove it.
  • Clear removes added text/signatures you haven't saved yet.
  • Save downloads the edited PDF.
